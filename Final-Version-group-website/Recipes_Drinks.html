Banana & Oat Breakfast Smoothie

Serves : 1

Preparation time : 5 mins

Nutritious cheap smoothie that sets you up for the day.

  • Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Medjool dates (optional)
  • 1 handful of Spinach
  • 2 handfuls of Frozen Berries
  • 1/2 a cup of Almond Milk
  • 3 tbsp Oats
  • 1 tbsp Hemp Powder
  • 1 tbsp Chia Seeds

  • Equipment

  • Blender
  • Knife
  • Tablespoon

  1. Method

  2. Peel and chop the Banana
  3. Pip the Dates
  4. Blend until smooth

Creamy Mango & Coconut Smoothie

Serves : 1

Preparation time : 5 mins

A smooth tangy drink with a hint of coconut.

  • Ingredients

  • 200ml (1/2 tall glass) Coconut Milk
  • 4 tbsp Coconut Milk Yogurt
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 tbsp ground Flaxseed
  • 1 tbsp Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Sunflower Seeds
  • 120g (1/4 bag) Frozen Mango Chunks
  • 1 Passion Fruit(optional)

  • Equipment

  • Tall Glass
  • Tablespoon
  • Knife
  • Blender

  1. Method

  2. Measure out all ingredients
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Scrape passion fruit seeds on top (if using)

Minted Fruit Cocktail Juice

Serves : 1

Preparation time : 5 mins

A slightly different take on the norm but the mint works very well with the fruit.

  • Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 1 Orange
  • 1/2 cup of Pineapple
  • 2 cups Watermelon
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
  • A small sprig of Fresh Mint

  • Equipment

  • Apple Peeler
  • Apple Corer
  • Knife
  • Blender
  • Teaspoon
  • Juicer

  1. Method

  2. Juice the Orange
  3. Juice the Apple
  4. Juice the Pineapple
  5. Transfer Juice and other Ingredients to Blender
  6. Blend until Smooth
