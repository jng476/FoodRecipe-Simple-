About Us

About Us

We are a group of four students currently studying Computing courses at Dundee University.

In our Web Authoring module we were tasked with using our knowledge of HTML and CSS to create a website which provides information to a group/community of people.

We chose to dedicate our site to the Student community and specifically the food which Students consume.

We wanted to offer Students a site to visit where they can find various healthy and relatively easy to make recipes for Starters, Main Courses, Desserts and Drinks.
